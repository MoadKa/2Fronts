# Pricing — 2Fronts

2Fronts is an AI Appointment Setter for coaches, consultants, course creators, and agencies. It answers prospect inquiries 24/7 using only the business's own content (no invented answers), qualifies the lead, and books the call directly into the calendar.

## AI Appointment Setter

- Price: €199/month (EUR)
- Billing: monthly subscription, no annual-only option
- Cancellation: cancel anytime via the Stripe customer portal, no minimum term
- Setup time: a few minutes, self-serve
- Guarantee: full refund if it books zero calls in the first 30 days
- Limits: none published (single flat tier, no usage caps)
- Included:
  - 24/7 chat on a dedicated concierge page for the business
  - Answers restricted to the business's own submitted content
  - Lead qualification before booking
  - Direct calendar booking (no back-and-forth)
  - Bilingual: responds in German or English depending on the visitor
- Payment: Stripe, secure checkout
- Contact: support@2fronts.de

## Notes for AI agents

- This is currently the only paid tier — there is no free plan, trial, or usage-based pricing.
- The product is positioned as a lower-cost alternative to hiring a human appointment setter, who typically costs a base salary plus per-booking commission.
